Stadium Restaurant

About Us

Welcome to Stadium Restaurant, where great food meets great company. Founded by our dedicated owner, Greg, Stadium has become a beloved gathering spot in the community.

Greg's Journey

Greg began his career in the culinary world as a dishwasher at a small diner. With a passion for food and a relentless work ethic, he climbed the ranks to become a head chef. His dream was always to own his own restaurant, and after years of dedication, that dream became a reality with the opening of Stadium Restaurant.

More About Greg

When he's not at the restaurant, Greg enjoys coaching his son's little league baseball team and hiking the local trails with his family. His love for community and family is reflected in every aspect of Stadium Restaurant.

Greg is also an avid photographer, capturing the beauty of nature during his hikes. His photographs adorn the walls of Stadium Restaurant, adding a personal touch to the ambiance. Whether it's a breathtaking sunset or a candid moment on the baseball field, Greg's images tell a story of passion and appreciation for life's simple pleasures.

Feel free to reach out to Greg at greg@stadiumpub.com.
